# The Future of Decor TV

We are proud to announce the official launch of **Möba TV**, the first Tunisian IPTV channel dedicated exclusively to furniture, design, and architecture.

## A 360° Partnership
Developed in co-production with **Cerise Com**, this project goes beyond simple video production. We built the channel identity, the broadcast infrastructure, and the content strategy.

Hosted by architect **Asma Jerbi**, the channel serves as a visual catalog for trends and inspiration.
